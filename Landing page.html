WHAT IS EBOOK?

Ebook is short for "electronic book," and uses either a computer, mobile device, or ebook reader to display long-form texts in book form. Ebooks have multiple digital "pages" that people can navigate through, and are often packaged as a PDF document so they can easily be sent from one user to another.

MORE FACT TO KNOW ABOUT EBOOK

There are some key differences between this self-publishing (which, by the way, doesn’t have the negative connotations it used to) and the traditional publishing process. You won’t be dealing with printed books, for one. That eliminates the expense and hassle of actually creating books, storing them, and delivering them — and that may not even sell. It’s going to be all digital.
